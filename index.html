Data & Attribution

This database compiles experimental and computational insights across selected phosphide semiconductor families. The computational work is based on high-throughput DFT screening using the HSE06 functional performed by Zhenkun Yuan. All PL measurements were performed and analyzed by Smitakshi Goswami. BaCd₂P₂ PL measurements were performed by Gideon Kassa.
