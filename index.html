Semiconducting Materials Families

This site organizes key materials systems investigated for photovoltaic and infrared optoelectronic applications. Each section contains crystal structure information, photoluminescence data, band gap values, and links to relevant papers.

AM₂P₂

Zintl-phase phosphides including Ca-, Sr-, and Ba-based compounds with strong relevance for next-generation absorber design.

Zinc phosphides

ZnP₂ and related zinc phosphide systems with promising optical properties and earth-abundant compositions.

MgP₄

Magnesium phosphide family with interesting structure–property relationships and potential semiconducting behavior.

SrI₂P₂

Strontium-containing phosphide systems of interest for benchmarking theory, crystal chemistry, and optoelectronic properties.

Rb/NaZn₄P₃

Alkali zinc phosphides offering a broader structural and chemical design space for new semiconductor discovery.
