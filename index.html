Data & Attribution

This database compiles experimental and computational insights across selected phosphide semiconductor families. The computational work is the result of a high-throughput screening of ~40,000 materials using first-principles DFT calculations with the HSE06 functional performed by Zhenkun Yuan. All photoluminescence (PL) measurements presented here were performed and analyzed by Smitakshi Goswami as part of ongoing research on next-generation photovoltaic and infrared materials. The BaCd₂P₂ PL measurements were performed by Gideon Kassa.

This materials database was developed and curated by Smitakshi Goswami. All photoluminescence (PL) measurements presented here were performed and analyzed by the author.
